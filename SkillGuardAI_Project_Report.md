## 2. INTRODUCTION
SkillGuard AI is a comprehensive, intelligent platform designed to revolutionize the recruitment process through automated skill validation. By utilizing advanced Artificial Intelligence, the system provides a seamless experience for both recruiters seeking top-tier talent and students looking to benchmark and improve their professional skills.

**Purpose:**
To provide an objective, data-driven environment where skills are evaluated based on performance rather than just credentials.

**Problem Solved:**
- Eliminates manual screening burnout for HR teams.
- Reduces unconscious bias in recruitment.
- Provides candidates with immediate growth-oriented feedback.

---

## 3. OBJECTIVES
- **Automate skill assessment:** Utilize AI to grade technical and soft skills instantly.
- **AI-Based Feedback:** Offer qualitative insights to help candidates understand their performance.
- **Efficient Hiring:** Support recruiters with aggregated analytics and candidate comparison tools.
- **Continuous Improvement:** Bridge the skill gap through personalized learning roadmaps.

---

## 4. SYSTEM OVERVIEW
The architecture of SkillGuard AI is divided into two primary modules:
1. **Student Mode:** A personalized dashboard for taking tests, checking results, and following career roadmaps.
2. **Recruiter Mode:** A management suite for creating assessments, monitoring candidate progress, and making data-backed hiring decisions.

## 7. STUDENT FEATURES (DETAILED)

### A. Assessment Module
- **AI Skill Test:** Dynamic technical challenges with AI-driven scoring.
- **AI Interview:** Conversational technical evaluation using LLM processing.
- **AI Proctored Interview:** Mandatory assessments assigned by recruiters with active monitoring.
- **Proctored MCQ Exam:** Time-sensitive technical quizzes.
- **CV Upload Assessment:** AI analyzes resumes to generate tailored practice questions.

### B. Proctoring System
- **Violation Detection:** Flags tab switching, window resizing, and screen sharing.
- **Session Control:** Prevents cheating by auto-submitting or terminating tests after multiple violations.

### C. Confidence Checker
Evaluates soft skills through behavioral questioning, focusing on:
- **Confidence:** Tone and certainty of language.
- **Fluency:** Coherence and flow of responses.
- **Communication:** Clarity and structural soundness.

### D. Analytics Section
- **AI breakdown:** Granular scoring across various domains.
- **Skill Gap Analysis:** Identifying exactly where the student needs improvement.
- **Personalized Roadmap:** Custom learning paths generated based on weak areas.
- **Career AI Suggestions:** Suggestions for roles best suited to current skills.

### E. Dashboard Features
- **Notifications:** Real-time alerts for test assignments and results.
- **Profile Management:** Centralized hub for updating skills and contact info.

## 12. WORKING FLOW
1. **Onboarding:** User registers and selects their role (Student/Recruiter).
2. **Assessment:** Student takes a test or an interview (AI evaluates in real-time).
3. **Data Processing:** The AI engine analyzes responses for accuracy, confidence, and integrity.
4. **Insights Generation:** Dashboards update with scores, gap analysis, and roadmaps.
5. **Loop Closure:** Recruiters view analytics for hiring; Students follow roadmaps for growth.
