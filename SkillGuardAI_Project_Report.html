Introduction

SkillGuard AI is a state-of-the-art recruitment and skill assessment platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence to bridge the gap between talented candidates and modern recruiters. By automating the evaluation process, the platform ensures objective, scalable, and data-driven hiring decisions.

In the traditional hiring landscape, manual screening is time-consuming and often prone to human bias. SkillGuard AI solves this by introducing proctored AI assessments, automated CV analysis, and personalized learning roadmaps, creating a comprehensive ecosystem for career growth and talent acquisition.

The system is architected as a dual-module ecosystem designed to serve both ends of the recruitment funnel:

1. Student Mode

A personal career hub where students can take assessments, track their progress, and receive AI-guided mentorship.

2. Recruiter Mode

A powerful management dashboard for HR professionals to create exams, monitor candidates, and analyze talent pools.

Student Features (Detailed)

A. Assessment Module

Our AI-powered testing suite provides a multi-dimensional view of a candidate's capabilities:

  • AI Skill Test: Subject-specific challenges with real-time AI grading.
  • AI Interview: A conversational interface that evaluates verbal responses to technical questions.
  • CV Upload Assessment: Analyzes uploaded resumes against job roles to suggest interview preparation.
  • Assigned Exams: Mandatory proctored tests assigned directly by potential recruiters.

B. Proctoring System

To ensure academic integrity, we implement a robust proctoring layer:

  • Detects tab-switching and window minimize events.
  • Monitors for screen violations and multiple displays.
  • Automatically submits or terminates the session upon repeated violations.

C. Confidence Checker

Beyond technical skills, the AI evaluates soft skills by asking behavioral questions and analyzing:

  • Fluency: The flow and coherence of speech/text.
  • Confidence: The tone and certainty in responses.
  • Communication: Clarity and structural quality of the delivery.

D. Analytics Section

Data visualization is used to display a deep dive into candidate performance:

Performance Breakdown

Visual charts showing scores across different skill domains (e.g., Coding, Logic, Communication).

Skill Gap Analysis

Identifies exact areas where the candidate fell short of industry standards.

AI Suggestions: The system provides contextual career path recommendations based on current strengths.

E. Dashboard Features

  • Notification Hub: Real-time alerts for new exams, results, or messages.
  • Profile Management: Ability to update skills, contact info, and portfolio links.
  • Activity Feed: Timeline of recent assessments and achievements.
